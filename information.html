Rules

EpicVerse Server rules.

RULES
Clean Game
No profanity, spamming, excessive caps, English only in global chat (use /r or /msg for other languages)
No inappropriate skins, names, builds, signs, etc.
As with the 'keeping safe online' Do not engage in inappropriate behaviour on any platform. Please report it if you have concerns.
Pvp'ing without consent not allowed, in a pvp arenas.
Tp trap killing or death camping is not allowed.
No Hacked Clients
Any mod that gives you an advantage is not allowed
Hacks like; X-ray, Flying, Speed, etc are not allowed.
Do not use autoclickers in PvP
Do not abuse glitches
Duping, cheating, etc. Please report and you may even get a reward ;)
Nether Roof Building is strictly prohibited
Glitch onto the Nether Roof and building there is not allowed.
Server Griefing
No lava casts, encasing, flooding nor world, nether or end griefing
No trolling
Harassment, scamming, encouraging harm, trapping
Don't discuss punishments. Don't ban/mute evade with other accounts.
No inappropriate content
Referencing or talking about vulgar, sexual or drug topics etc.
Names, skins, banners, builds, item names, etc.
Do not impersonate staff
Name, colour, rank etc.
Do not leak personal information
Leaking personal information without the person's permission such as full names, addresses and any other personally identifiable information
No AFKing at areas which gives you an advantage
Auto-farms, grinders, etc.
No AFK devices
Pools etc
No Zero Tick/Flying machines
We may also ask you to remove any large areas which cause server lag
Please be efficient with your redstone machines

TOWNY AGE
Poaching of players from other towns, or towns from other nations is not allowed
Players and Towns must make the first approach!
Raiding of unclaimed bases/bankrupt towns is allowed, but not griefing them
All block changes are recorded by the server
Donating/giving away ranked perks or donator items is not allowed
Donators cannot give away donor perks and items, nor donor perks to players who are not the same rank.
Ranked perks in-game cannot be given used for other players that have not achieved the ranked perks.
Donors and ranked players may not give/sell/use perks and benefits for other players
This covers in-games ranks and donor ranks.
Tools, armour etc cannot be used/given to non-ranked players
There are strict limits on hoppers, chests, item frames, spawners etc.

Town System

Towny Plugin command list.

/towny
Shows basic Towny commands.

? – Shows more Towny commands.
map – Shows the Towny map.
prices – Shows taxes/costs associated with running a town.
time – Shows time until next new-day (tax/upkeep collection.)
top residents (all/town/nation) – Shows top residents.
top land (all/resident/town) – Shows top land owners.

/plot
Shows the /plot commands.

? – Shows more plot commands.
claim – Resident command to claims a plot
claim auto – claim plots that are for sale
unclaim – Resident command to unclaim personally owned plots.
unclaim circle/rect – unclaim owned plots in a circle or rectangle shape.
unclaim (# (radius around current position)) – Radius of area to unclaim.
forsale/fs [amt] – Set a plot for sale.
forsale/fs circle/rect – Set a shape.
forsale/fs (# (radius around current position)) – Radius of area to set for sale.
notforsale/nfs – Set a plot to not be for sale.
set reset – Sets a shop/embassy/arena/wilds plot back to a normal plot.
set shop – Sets a plot to a shop plot.
set embassy – Sets a plot to an embassy plot.
